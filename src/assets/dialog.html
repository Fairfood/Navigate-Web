Tree cover loss events describe the reduction of tree canopy due to factors like deforestation, natural disasters, urban development, and illegal logging. This leads to significant environmental impacts, including habitat destruction, biodiversity loss, and increased carbon emissions, affecting both local and global climates. Addressing tree cover loss is vital for environmental conservation and climate change mitigation.
